Introduction

PillCheckr ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application PillCheckr (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account preferences

Health Information

To provide pill identification services, we may collect:

  • Photos of pills you upload or capture (sent to AI services for processing, NOT stored beyond your session)
  • Pill identification history (text results and pill information, NOT the actual photos)
  • Saved medication information
  • Search queries

Important: Photos you take or upload are transmitted to third-party AI services (Google Gemini AI, Google ML Kit) for analysis and text recognition. These services process your images to identify text and markings on pills. The photos are NOT stored in our database beyond your current session - only the identification results (text descriptions) are saved to your history.

Device Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App usage statistics

Camera and Photo Library Access

PillCheckr requires access to your device camera and photo library to:

  • Capture photos of pills for identification
  • Allow you to select existing photos for identification
  • Process images using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and AI analysis

How Photos are Processed:

  • Photos are first processed locally on your device when possible (using ML Kit)
  • Photos may also be sent to Google Gemini AI cloud service for advanced analysis and text recognition
  • Images are used solely for pill identification purposes
  • We do not use your photos for marketing or advertising
  • We do not sell your photos to third parties

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide pill identification services using image recognition and database lookup
  • Maintain your account and provide user authentication
  • Store your identification history for future reference
  • Improve our database and identification accuracy
  • Send notifications about app updates or important information
  • Provide customer support when you contact us
  • Analyze app usage to improve functionality and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud or unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Your account information and identification history (text results, not photos) are stored securely using Supabase (our cloud database provider)
  • Some data may be cached locally on your device for offline access
  • Pill photos you upload or capture are:
    • Sent to Google Gemini AI and/or Google ML Kit for image processing and text recognition
    • Temporarily processed and cached by these services during identification
    • Not stored by PillCheckr beyond your current app session
    • Deleted from our systems when you close the app or complete the identification
    • May be temporarily retained by third-party AI services (Google) according to their retention policies
    • Only the identification results (text descriptions, pill information) are saved to your history, not the actual photos

Important: Currently, we do NOT store your pill photos beyond your active session. In the future, we may add optional features to save photos to your identification history for future reference. If we implement photo storage, we will:

  • Update this privacy policy and notify you
  • Make photo storage optional (opt-in)
  • Provide controls to delete stored photos
  • Request your consent before enabling this feature

How We Protect Your Data

  • All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Passwords are encrypted using secure hashing algorithms
  • Access to databases is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

  • Customer support services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide PillCheckr's functionality. Your data may be shared with these services as described below:

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

We use Supabase for cloud database services, user authentication, and data storage.

  • Data Shared: Account information, pill identification history, user preferences
  • Purpose: Secure data storage and account management
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Google Gemini AI (Image Analysis)

We use Google Gemini AI for advanced optical character recognition (OCR) and pill image analysis.

  • Data Shared: Photos of pills that you upload or capture are sent to Google's Gemini AI service for text extraction and analysis
  • Purpose: To identify text, numbers, and markings on pills for accurate identification
  • Processing: Images are processed through Google's cloud servers
  • Retention: Google may temporarily cache your images as described in their privacy policy
  • Training Data: According to Google's policies, data sent to Gemini API is not used to train or improve Google's models
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Important: By using PillCheckr's image identification features, you consent to your pill photos being processed by Google's AI services

Google ML Kit (Text Recognition - Fallback)

We also use Google ML Kit for on-device text recognition as a fallback option.

  • Data Shared: Text recognition processing may occur locally on your device or through Google's services
  • Purpose: Backup OCR method if primary AI service is unavailable
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Note: You cannot opt out of these third-party services while using the pill identification features, as they are essential to the app's core functionality. If you do not wish to share your data with these services, please do not use the image identification features.
